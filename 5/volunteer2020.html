Volunteering in our time

The volunteer movement in Russia 2020 is experiencing a new upsurge. More and more people understand the need for personal participation in solving the problems facing society and the state, and are ready to devote their time to this free of charge, use their experience and knowledge. A web portal DOBRO.RU for volunteers and organizations was created especially for this purpose in Russia.

Volunteers took an active part in the fight against the pandemic, along with doctors and social workers. Participation in the fight against coronavirus infection (COVID-19) on the basis of volunteerism has found its form within the framework of the All-Russian Mutual Assistance Campaign #МыВместе (#We're Together). It started on March 21, 2020 almost simultaneously with the announcement of the self-isolation regime. The initiators of the action were the All-Russian People's Front, the Association of Volunteer Centers and the All-Russian public movement "Medical Volunteers".

Volunteer regional headquarters of the All-Russian project #МыВместе (#We're Together) continue their work. They focus on several areas: the work of volunteers in call centers at clinics and regional ministries of health, the delivery of medicines and products to older people and those who are quarantined and outpatient treatment, the transportation of doctors and health workers to work and on calls.

Today, no major event can do without the help of volunteers in the Arkhangelsk region. They organize all-Russian actions and local social projects.

The main types of volunteering:

1. Social volunteering

2. Environmental volunteering

3. Media volunteering

4. Sports volunteering

5. Event volunteering
